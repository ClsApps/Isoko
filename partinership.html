World Isoko Ibohora Pentecostal Mission

World Isoko Ibohora is a Christian Ministry that is based on Pentecostal foundation. The ministry is operating exclusively for religious, educational, health, benevolent and development purposes in many parts of the country and also the far world.

Our mission is to reach the people of God who are still in darkness by taking to them light and hope in Christ Jesus and be set free by the power of his cross.

The memorandum of understanding intends to establish the basic terms of agreement to create both friendship and development of our ministry and our partner.
It is a binding agreement between us and our partner that agree to work together in terms of spearheading the gospel, social, economic and technical advice and any other role which can help us and our partner to grow both physically and spiritually.
  • We agree to obey and respect the advice intending to build of which our partner will prove.

  • We will follow principles and regulations found in our ministry’s constitution. But not distorting our partner’s constitution.

  • We are independent on our own property in administration, Finances, and cultures.

  • To support or sponsor both in spiritual matters and physical matters,

  • To do advocacy among other sponsors including the government and the whole world.

  • To help in the ordination ceremonies of ministry specifically for the senior Pastor and Bishops only.

  • To b both advisors and consultants in various church issues specifically intending to cause development of the ministry.

  • The agreement of partnership or memorandum of understanding is termed as a friendship (working) agreement between two parties.

  • The memorandum of understanding is protected by co-strict confidentiality as agreed up on and decided between the two parties and during their working in Uganda and the whole world.

  • Commencement and duration: The memorandum of understanding will be valid right away from the day and date on which it is signed up on by both parties, and it will remain in force as long as the two parties are still serving the lord.

  • Any activity which will be made between these two parties, a report will be done in transparency with Bank statements, receipts and other documents.

  • Any activities taking place between the two parties will be documented and any problems arise between the two parties will be solved through the discussions and reconciliation between the two parties.

  • Final availability: Any political-administrative approach, any pursuit of expertise will be done on agreed up on terms between the two parties in order to work towards the kingdom of God to achieve a common goal.

  • The memorandum of understanding can be stopped if any serious offences are found in any of the two parties and a written document must be provided to prove the truth and reason on which the offence is based up on .
