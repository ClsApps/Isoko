About Ministry

BACKGROUND

World Isoko Ibohora Ministry is a registered non-political and not for profit making ministry operating exclusively for religious, to take care and support orphans, widows and vulnerable peoples in educational, health, benevolent and development purposes. It started in 2009 by three (3) people and currently has members over 23 branches in different areas in Uganda.

    FOUNDER

    Bishop Maso Rwizihirwa John is a Prophet, Apostolic Bishop, founder and presiding overseer of World Isoko Ibohora Ministries since 2009. He is married to Reverend Nyaremezo Antoinette Maso since 1991, she is a great woman and good servant of God. He is a biological father of eight children, five (5) sons and three daughters.

    In 2009, he was appointed and is the minister of evangelism in Kampala region for national fellowship of born-again Pentecostal churches in Uganda as a member. In 2012, he was the international coordinator and overseer of World Isoko Source of Deliverance, miracles, Pentecostal mission in Uganda. In 2014, it was his gratitude to be ordained Bishop and founder of World Isoko Source of Deliverance ministries (World Isoko Ibohora Ministries).
